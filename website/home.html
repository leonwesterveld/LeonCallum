    • Nationaal Videogame Museum

    The National Video Game Museum is 'the place to be' for everyone who wants to know (and experience) more about history, social and cultural side of video games. The museum also lets its visitors experience the games of tomorrow. Gamers young and old are welcome to play, explore, learn and share experiences online.

    history

    It was found in 2011 on the Cobaltstraat in Zoetermeer. There Hasan decided to rent the location with 4 friends and the adventure could grow into their own meeting place to practice their hobby and relax with a game.

    location

    The museum is located in the middle of the passage of Stadshart Zoetermeer, on the first floor at the top of the escalator. The V&D used to be located here.
